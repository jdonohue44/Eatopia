What to Expect from SMS

You’ll receive evidence-based prompts that support healthy eating decisions. Here are a few examples:

  • On grocery day: “70% of Eatopians who start shopping in the produce aisle eat 2x more fiber.”
  • Progress feedback: “You've logged three balanced meals this week. Great job!”
  • Photo tracking prompt: “Text a photo of your next meal to us and we’ll send an AI nutritionist insight back.”
  • Recipe Inspiration: “Check out this 5-minute oatmeal recipe, full of nutrients and fiber to start your day [image].”
  • Quick Tips: “If you’re reaching for a snack late at night, pause. Are you hungry or just tired or thirsty?”
  • Community: “83% of Eatopians added greens to their plate this week.”
